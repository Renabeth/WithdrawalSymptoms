## Withdrawal Symptoms

A visual novel by Studio GKG created for the [Toxic Yuri VN Jam 2 ](https://itch.io/jam/toxic-yuri-vn-jam-2) 

**Can you begin to mourn when you haven't realized what you've lost?**

**Withdrawal Symptoms** is a psychological yuri visual novel about drugs and the consequences of ignoring reality. When Evelyn meets a mourning girl named Valerie, their lives are forever changed as Evelyn spirals down a path of jealousy and loathing. The game features multiple paths and endings for the player to experience the different ways Evelyn and Valerie's story could have gone.
